About Events

1. Dance Competitions


	* Dance Competitions consists of teams competing with each other in team of 10-12 members each.
	* Teams need to choreograph according to a theme. 
	* Teams with more than 12 members won't be allowed. 
	* The time limit for dance is 5 minutes.

	Register for Dance Competition

2. Music Competitions


	* Music Competitions will consist of teams competing with each other in team of 2 members each, in first round. 
	* 4 teams out of 8 will go in next round, i.e. Buzzer round.
	* 2 teams consisting of 4 students will go in final round.
	* Student needs to prepare a song, Karaoke is allowed. 
	* The time limit is 3 minutes.

	Register for Music Competition

2. Art It Out


	* This is will consist of painting out your thoughts. 
	* A topic will be given on the spot. students have to draw/paint/sketch their imagination on paper.
	* It is an individual participation event.
	* Students have to bring their own material. Only A3 size paper will be provided. 
	* The time limit is 1 hour.

	Register for Art It Out

2. Fashion Show


	* This event is ONLY for students in FINAL YEARS. 
	* Each group should give a theme to their respective coordinators.
	* Team size can be 10-18 members.
	* The track should be submitted whenever asked. 
	* The time limit is 35 minutes.
	* Props which can be carried are allowed.

	Register for Fashion Show
